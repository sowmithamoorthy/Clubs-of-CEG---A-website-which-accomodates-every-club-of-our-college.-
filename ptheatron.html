WELCOME TO THEATRON

The official theater and drama club of Anna University, started in 2006 with actor Amitash Pradhan (VIP) as one of the co-founders.
Theatron was initiated in the hope of inspiring creative ideas and celebrations the artform.. The club has also collaborated with other theater organisations like Crea-Shakthi and won prizes in prestigious college-level competitions. Exciting weekly meets which include games, workshops and discussions are conducted with the 50+ member network.
