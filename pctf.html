About CEG Tech Club

CEG Tech Forum is a gathering place for young people who are passionate about technology and tirelessly work to advance the student community so that it can take the next step in the field. It was established in 2006 and is entirely run and managed by students with the fervent goal of transforming original concepts and differing viewpoints into clever and innovative solutions to today's pressing issues. CTF has different domains, and the sum of the contributions from all of its teams is what gives it the strength it possesses. The foundations of idea sharing, teamwork, collaboration, and cooperation among all of its members support this student-led organization. With the assistance of all of these, CTF persistently strives for expansion and proliferation.
